The Earth's Heartbeat

Exhibited at:

Hum of Existence, Catalyst Berlin

Role:

Project Management/Visual Design

Year:

2024

Team:

Diya Bhattacharjee, Elena Dimitrova, Nicola Bisset

Schumann resonances are a set of electromagnetic waves referred to as Earth's "heartbeat"—that are trapped and excited within the atmospheric cavity between the Earth's surface and the ionosphere by lightning.

Using live data from Schumann Resonance, we created an immersive installation with projection mapping, sound design and reflective material for the universe to play.
