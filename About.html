I am a Berlin-based New Media Artist & Researcher that blends user-centric research with XR and data-driven storytelling. My practice is rooted in the belief that technology should be as intentional, inclusive and care-filled, ensuring that every digital interaction is crafted with precision and purpose.

My work centers around the intersection of design, technology, and storytelling. I specialize in creating immersive experiences that bridge the gap between physical and digital worlds, using tools like Unity, Touchdesigner, and web technologies.

I believe in design as a medium for social change and heritage preservation. Whether it's through augmented reality platforms or digital archives, my goal is to create meaningful connections between users and the narratives that shape our culture.
