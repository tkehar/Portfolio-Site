Architecture Design Art Magazine

Editorial design has always been a passion of mine. This is why I took on the role of designer at Architecture, Design, Art (ADA) Magazine. ADA is a bi-monthly publication that archives and celebrates creative work in Pakistan and neighbouring regions. In 2018 the publication used its platform to advocate for urban regeneration with a dialogue series “Do You Know Your City?” and conferences like “The Future of Heritage”. In my capacity as a designer I lead a team to develop layouts for the publication and structured systems for the planning and documentation of their events. Through this work experience I also developed my ability to write about design and art.
